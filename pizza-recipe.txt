Ingredients:
 - 1 wrap
 - mozzarella cheese
 - tomato paste
 - 1 capsicum
 - 1 onion
 - feta cheese
 - pepperoni

Directions: 
 1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
 
 2. Cover wrap with tomato paste
 
 3. In this order add mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, capsicum, onion, feta cheese
 
 4. Cook in oven for 10 - 15 minutes.
